Interactive lab

Rocket. Combustion. CFD.

A lightweight simulation corner built into the homepage. Switch modes and trigger the scene to get a small piece of motion instead of a static description.

Rocket launch

A small launch sequence with a waiting rocket, engine glow, and a triggered ascent.

  • Idle flame flicker before launch
  • Click once to send it upward
  • Built as a lightweight visual, not a full game

Combustion plume

A dark chamber with rising heat, burning particles, and a pulse that intensifies the flame.

  • Animated heat field and sparks
  • Click to inject a stronger burst
  • Closer to a visual thought than a literal solver

CFD flow field

Particles stream past an obstacle and bend into a simplified wake pattern behind it.

  • Continuous particle advection
  • Click to add a stronger disturbance
  • Meant to suggest the feeling of a flow field

Research

Current focus areas.

The work sits at the intersection of computational fluid dynamics, multiphase flow, and reactive flow simulation.

Interface tracking

Numerical methods for moving interfaces in complex two-phase systems.

Two-phase flow simulation

Modeling and simulation of multiphase dynamics where interface behavior matters.

Detonation wave modeling

High-speed reactive flows with strong gradients and challenging wave structures.
